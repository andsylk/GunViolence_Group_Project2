Interesting Observations:

  • The counties that experienced the highest number of deaths cause by gun violence are also among the most populous
    • Los Angeles County, CA: 5252 deaths vs 10.17 million residents
    • Cook County, IL: 5113 deaths vs 5.238 million residents
    • Wayne County, MI: 3044 deaths vs 1.759 million residents
    • Harris County, TX: 2984 deaths vs 4.538 million residents
    • Philadelphia County, PA: 2588 deaths vs 1.568 million residents
  • Approximately 30% of all gun related deaths occur in the summer months (June, July, and August)
  • Since 2014, the total number of gun deaths has been trending upwards
    • 12268 deaths in 2014
    • 14287 deaths in 2015
    • 15769 deaths in 2016
