Gun Ownership vs. Gun Deaths

(2013)

More Guns, More Deaths?

The chart below shows the gun ownership percentage and number of firearm deaths in 2013. Each marker represents a state in the U.S. The trendline shows a positive correlation indicating high levels of gun ownernship have larger numbers of gun related deaths.
